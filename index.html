I am a Ph.D. student in Prof. Kavulurus's Natural Language Processing group, Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky. Earlier, I received my bachelor degree at the Indiana University in dual majors of computer science (specialized in artificial intelligence) and mathematics.

My research interests include information retrieval, natural language processing, and machine learning.
